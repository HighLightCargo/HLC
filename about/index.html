About High Light Cargo

High Light Cargo has been in business since 2005, and has built multiple relationships with several logistics and delivery service companies. This has enabled us to always get YOU the, customer the best rate for your shipment.

Our customer service is top-notch, because our staff is our important solution to assisting our customers needs. They speak several languages including, Somali, English, Arabic, French, Pashto & Dari. That means when you come in to High Light Cargo you can always find someone to communicate with!

Over 15 years we have cultivated several business relationships with our customers ensuring they return back to us. This is because of our amazing service and dedication to getting them the best rate and sending their shipment wherever it needs to go around the world.
